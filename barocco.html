Experience the Elegance of Barocco

Barocco Shisha

Rich, bold, and unapologetically artistic - Barocco is more than a hookah; it's a statement of timeless luxury. Inspired by the ornamental elegance of classical furniture and interiors, this piece brings history and style into the present moment. Every line and curve speaks of Italian grandeur, capturing the opulence of the Baroque spirit with a modern twist.

Handcrafted Elegance

Hand-painted in vibrant yellows, deep black, and warm terracotta hues, Barocco takes inspiration from ceramic motifs found in Southern Italian furniture and architecture. The base and stem are crafted from natural red clay, then glazed and decorated entirely by hand - making each piece one of a kind. No two Barocco hookahs are ever exactly alike. A true fusion of art and function, it transforms any setting into a stage for beauty and indulgence.

Barocco Accessories

Barocco includes a set of matching ceramic accessories, all made from the same red clay and finished in the same dramatic design. It features a ceramic bowl for smooth, even heat, a custom hose handle wrapped in velvet for a luxurious grip, and a matching ceramic stem and base that blend seamlessly into the overall look. Built to perform but designed to impress, every detail of Barocco reflects our passion for craftsmanship and elegance.
